Creamy Fettuccine Carbonara


This recipes prepares a delicious plate of Creamy Fettuccine Carbonara made with pancetta, eggs, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 1/4 pound pancetta
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Cook the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, heavy cream, and butter in a bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the pancetta.
  5. Remove the pan from the heat and add the egg mixture to the pasta.
  6. Stir the pasta until the sauce thickens.
  7. Season with parsley and black pepper.
