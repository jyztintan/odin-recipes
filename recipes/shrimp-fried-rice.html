Shrimp Fried Rice


This recipes prepares a delicious plate of Shrimp Fried Rice made with shrimp, eggs, and vegetables. This dish was inspired by the Din Tai Fung style but now in a homemade style, perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup green onions
  • 1/4 cup peas
  • 1/4 cup carrots
  • 1/4 cup corn
  • 1/4 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1/4 cup seaweed

Instructions

  1. Cook the shrimp in a pan until pink.
  2. Whisk together the eggs in a bowl.
  3. Heat the sesame oil and vegetable oil in a pan.
  4. Add the eggs to the pan and scramble until cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan and stir until heated through.
  6. Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, green onions, peas, carrots, corn, bamboo shoots, and seaweed to the pan and cook until soft.
  7. Stir in the cooked shrimp.
  8. Season with salt and pepper.
